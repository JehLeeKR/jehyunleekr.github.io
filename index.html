About Me

Security researcher/engineer. I am currently working for LevelBlue/Trustwave, a world-leading Managed Security Service Provider, as a senior security researcher.

For the last five years in the industrial position, I have spearheaded the AI/ML research team, responsible for developing incident detection, data analysis, and automation systems for EDR/SIEM using machine learning (ML), deep learning (DL), and natural language processing (NLP) techniques.

My research experiences continue in academia and industry. I have worked in research teams at SMU and NUS in Singapore, as well as at KAIST and Korea University in Korea, in senior and leading positions. After entering the industry, I continued my research and published papers on emerging security topics in AI for security.
